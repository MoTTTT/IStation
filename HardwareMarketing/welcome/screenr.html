Screen Resolution

The resolution of your screen can be changed by simultaneously pressing the CTRL, ALT and + keys (<CTRL><ALT>+) or <CTRL><ALT>-. This has the effect of "zooming in" on a portion of the screen. The virtual screen stays the same size, but the visible portion becomes a different size. Other portions of the virtual screen may be viewed by moving the mouse pointer to the edge of your screen.
