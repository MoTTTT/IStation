The Task Bar

At the bottom of the screen, streching the from the left of the screen to the right is the Task Bar. The left hand side of the bar is illustrated below.

Clicking on the Start button on the left hand side of your Task Bar causes the Start Menu to appear. This menu contains a sub-set of the options available in the Window Manager's Main Menu, which is also available by left-clicking on the desktop.

The right hand side of the bar contains a small clock.

In between the Start button and the clock is a button representing each open window. The buttons contain the icon of the program that opened the window, and the window's title. A window is maximised (i.e. displayed on the desktop) by left-clicking on the button representing the button in question.
