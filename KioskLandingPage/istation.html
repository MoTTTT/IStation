Internet Workstation
 

Products:

  • Internet Browsing
    Access to the World Wide Web by means of a Netscape browser interface. The client will be billed per 15 minute usage block at a rate of R5.00 per block.
  • Offline usage

    • Workstation not connected to the Internet and used for a wide variety of Linux based XWindows applications e.g. the Gimp which is a graphical design package, Blender which is animation software.  The client will be billed per 15 minute usage block at a rate of R4.00 per block.
     

  • Printing (Black & White)

    • Black and white printing available at a cost of R1.00 per A4 page, if the user wishes to print on paper sizes other than A4, the user will have to provide the paper and ensure that it conforms to the specifications for the printer to be used (HP 690C DeskJet).
     

  • Printing (Colour)

    • Colour printing is available at a cost of R1.50 per A4 page, if the user wishes to print on paper sizes other than A4, the user will have to provide the paper and ensure that it conforms to the specifications for the printer to be used (HP 690C DeskJet).
