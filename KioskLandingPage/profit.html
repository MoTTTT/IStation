Profit Distribution
 
  • Three parties participate in profit sharing:
    • The site operation (owner of the venue)
    • The machine owner (capital, non-executive)
    • The system owner (support services, distribution)
  • Profits are to be shared equally between these 3 parties.

    •  
  • Profits to be distributed monthly.
  • Profits to be calculated, first based on estimated expenditure until accounting includes current stock only.
  • Turnover to be collected periodically (weekly).
  • Trainer to be paid R7 cash for each  0.5hr session on receipt from customer (assumes R25/0.5hr training session to be updated).
