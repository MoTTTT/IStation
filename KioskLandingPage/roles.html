Detailed Description of Roles and Responsibilities

Responsibilities:

  • Site management
    • contractual agreements
    • staff management (site) - assign each of the other site responcibilities
    • profit distribution site signoff
      • site management keep one copy of distribution sign-off form
    • manage site concessions
      • a workstation account to be made available at the discretion of the site manager
      • at specified daily period
      • specified number of prints at cost
      • specified internet usage at cost
      • specifications to be negotiated with the program manager from time to time
  • Operations management should be on site at all times
    • manage bookkeeper staff
    • stock take, ordering
      • notify Q Solutions in advance when running low on consumables i.e. stiffies and paper
    • cashing up, cross checking
      • booking sheet entries should be matched by sales log entries
    • signing off booking sheets & sales sheet
      • sign off delivery of booking sheets and sales logs
      • on receipt of payment from client signature entered as validation
    • contacting system support when required - all support calls will be logged
      • cost of system malfunction support to be for the account of Q Solutions to be refunded at month end
      • cost of system operation queries to be for the account of the venue
    • turnover transfer sign-off
      • operations management keep one copy of transfer sign-off form
    • marking up trainer availability
      • make sure that booking sheets for two days in advance are available, mark the times that are available for training in highlighter.
  • Support manager
    • user training - prebooked or during predefined available time slots
    • user support - during predefined available time slots
      • Introducing users to the webbrowser
        • search engines
        • following links
        • downloading files and saving to disk
        • editing files
        • printing
      • Introduction to e-mail
      • Introducing users to Internet Relay Chat
    • bookkeeper training
    • bookkeeper support
  • Web host
    • pricing
    • can explain menu to customers
    • take bookings
      • select the time slot on the appropriate booking sheet
      • explain the availability of user support and training
      • record product as internet browsing, offline or training
      • record name in time slot
      • fill in the cost of booked slots
      • take the payment of the first slot as deposit on booking
    • logging sales
      • indicate the quantity of the product sold
      • indicate the product description as
        • browsing, offline or training time slots
        • stiffies
        • printing
      • record clients name
      • record total cost of product
      • record sign off by the operator that payment has been received
    • connect and disconnect internet
      • open browser, get search engine, hotmail, wordprocessor
    • selling stiffies
    • selling printing
  • System manager
    • system support
    • turnover transfer signoff
    • system stock and maintenance
    • support training
    • 1st line operational training
    • remote systems administration and log checks
  • Program manager
    • operations design
    • planning
    • business support
    • contractual agreements
    • system support
    • log correlation
    • profit distribution
